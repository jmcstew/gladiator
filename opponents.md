# 10 Arena Opponents

## 1. Valerius "The Blooded" - Disgraced Centurion
- **Gender:** Male
- **Homeland:** Rome
- **Appearance:** Scars across face, missing left eye, worn Roman armor, red cloak
- **Level:** 3
- **Stats:** STR 15, AGI 10, END 18

**Backstory:** Once Rome's finest centurion, Valerius led a legion into disaster in Germania. Stripped of his rank and marked with shame, he now fights in the arena seeking death or redemption—whichever comes first.

**Pre-battle Dialogue:**
> "I have fought in legions you never knew existed, boy. Your weapons are toys compared to what I've seen."

**Victory Quote:**
> "Not bad... for a novice."

**Defeat Quote:**
> "The arena claims another... remember my face, because it will haunt your dreams."

## 2. Boudicca's Daughter - Celtic Fury
- **Gender:** Female
- **Homeland:** Gaul
- **Appearance:** Wild red hair, blue woad war paint, leather armor, twin daggers
- **Level:** 4
- **Stats:** STR 12, AGI 20, END 12

**Backstory:** Daughter of a Gaulish queen who died fighting Roman invaders. She was raised in the resistance, learning to fight before she could read. Now she sells her blade to anyone who pays, but never against her people.

**Pre-battle Dialogue:**
> "The Romans taught me one thing—nothing cuts deeper than betrayal. Shall we see what you've learned?"

**Victory Quote:**
> "Your blood feeds the earth. The earth is grateful."

**Defeat Quote:**
> "My mother would have laughed at you... before cutting your throat."

## 3. Sicarius - The Desert Viper
- **Gender:** Male
- **Homeland:** Egypt
- **Appearance:** Shaved head, golden tattoos, linen wrappings, curved poisoned daggers
- **Level:** 5
- **Stats:** STR 10, AGI 25, END 8

**Backstory:** Once a temple guardian who protected pharaoh's secrets. When the temple fell, he took its deadliest weapon: knowledge of poisons that kill in seconds. He fights not for glory, but for the coin to fund his endless wandering.

**Pre-battle Dialogue:**
> "Every breath you take in my presence is a gift I can revoke. Choose your next words carefully."

**Victory Quote:**
> "The sand will drink your blood, and no one will remember your name."

**Defeat Quote:**
> "The desert... takes us all... eventually..."

## 4. Kriemhild - The Frozen Shield

- **Stats:** STR 16, AGI 8, END 20

**Victory Quote:**

**Defeat Quote:**

- **Stats:** STR 18, AGI 12, END 15
